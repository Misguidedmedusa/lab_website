About the Lab

Established in 2009, the Virology Lab at Shiv Nadar University focuses on understanding viral biology through molecular and structural approaches. Our work spans hepatitis viruses and emerging viral pathogens.

Research Areas

HBV cccDNA Biology

Dr. Pooja Bhatia investigates mechanisms regulating hepatitis B virus cccDNA persistence.

HBV Entry Biology

Ishika Agrawal studies HBV PreS1 interaction with NTCP and novel entry inhibitors.

KFDV Structural Biology

Aas Mohd. investigates structural aspects of KFDV RNA-dependent RNA polymerase.
